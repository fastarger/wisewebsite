PRIVACY POLICY

This Discord bot is intended for users over 13 years old and is designed to read user messages only when a specific command is used or when the channel name is "chatbot". We value user privacy and are committed to protecting their personal information. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide to us.

Information Collection

When the specific command is used or the channel name is "chatbot", the bot collects text information and user identifiers to perform the requested action. We do not collect additional information such as location data or personal identification information.

Information Use

Messages are not collected. The bot does not sell, rent, or share information with third parties.

Information Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information we collect. We use standard industry security practices to protect the information we collect and store. However, we cannot guarantee the security of information transmitted over the Internet or electronically stored. By using the bot, you acknowledge and agree that the bot will read your messages in order to produce a response through GPT3.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may change this privacy policy at any time without notice. The latest version of the privacy policy will always be available on our website. We recommend that you periodically review this policy to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us through Discord or email. We will be happy to assist you.
